About Me

James is a graduate of the Massey University of Wellington’s College of Creative Arts, having recently completed a Master of Creative Enterprise in 2021. The research portion of James’ postgraduate thesis explored the affective value of non-concequential player choice in linear narrative games. This accompanied the development of narrative-driven mystery game Project: Mystic and a business plan, realised through the creation of worker’s cooperative Studio Witchwood.

In 2019 James completed a Bachelor of Creative Media Production, focusing his studies around game development and digital art. James is currently working through the development of a couple personal projects since finishing MCE, including the completion of Project: Mystic (with plans to release the game early next year) and teaching himself html and css, resulting in the creation of this website. James also edits videos for his brother Alex's painting youtube channel.
